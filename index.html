About Habitron

In today's prevalent sedentary lifestyle, students often struggle to maintain time management while studying, leading to a disregard for regular breaks and prolonged periods of inactivity, negatively impacting their overall well-being. Excessive reliance on snoozing alarms and a lack of motivation contribute to unhealthy lifestyle habits, hindering individuals from maintaining an active and productive daily routine. Physical activity during study sessions can help for example to enhance productivity and combat sedentary behavior-related health risks. Therefore we developed an extendable multipurpose tool for good habits to investigate a way to improve this easily.

We introduce an innovative approach to establishing and reinforcing these positive behaviors through the use of an extendable multipurpose tool for good habits with the current 3 main features: alarm clock, Pomodoro timer, and ambient measurement.Our research focuses on investigating the effectiveness of this tool in promoting behavior change and exploring the usefulness of dividing users into different profiles.
